ME303: Metrology and Measurement

Mechanical Engineering Department

Course Overview

This course covers the fundamental principles of precision measurement, calibration techniques, and advanced instruments used in mechanical and manufacturing engineering. You will learn how to ensure accuracy, analyze errors, and utilize modern tools like CMMs and laser interferometers to maintain quality control in industrial processes.
